RSS News Aggregator

This is an awesome news aggregator app, currently deployed at amazon app store with app name: RSS News Aggregator

The primary objective of the application is to not let any big news aggregator track your likes/dislikes and the websites you visit.

Some of the salient features of the application include:

  • Add rss feeds
  • Save news to the read later list
  • Save liked news
  • Backup to Google Drive & Restore from Google Drive
  • Peek at, description
